A SHORT STORY ABOUT CRISTIANO RONALDO

His soccer position is forward and seven is his jersey number. Cristiano Ronaldo is one of the greatest player of his generation.

Cristiano Ronaldo started playing football(soccer) at a very young age. By the age of ten, all he thought about was soccer. "All he wanted to do as a boy was play football," his godfather, Fernao Sousa, recalled for British reporters, adding, "He loved the game so much he'd miss meal or escape out of his bedroom window with a ball when he was supposed to be doing his homewoke." This proves that CR7 was and now has passion for what he do.
